Language Studies

In recent years, I've caught onto the joy of language learning. Connecting across cultures and thinking in another language have been a source of internal growth and shifted my own perspectives. Right now I'm working to hone my Portuguese and develop my Spanish.

Portuguese - Conversational

I spent five months in Brazil, three of which were spent staying with Brazilian families. It took longer than expected and was frustrating at times, but I eventually built up to conversational Portuguese, though group settings are still incredibly difficult. With a beautiful sound and cadence, I love speaking Portuguese whenever I have a chance. I'm hunting for friends in Denver that are interested in conversing with me!

Spanish - Survival

I studied Spanish in school but never embraced the subject or applied what I learned. I revisited the language before my travels to Spain, Nicaragua and Argentina but wasn't up to converational level in time. I've continued studying since and I'm now focused on shifting my studies from Duolingo to conversation.

Esperanto - Beginner

It's simple, it's logical, it's fun! I'm obsessed with the idea of Esperanto and it's logical construction. I started studying with Duolingo and YouTube videos last year but decided to put it on hold because I was spreading myself too thin.

Duolingo

The largest free language learning platform in the world. It is available both on desktop and mobile app. Material varies between listening, speaking, reading and writing.

Anki

A spaced-repetition flashcard program that tracks what you know and shows you the cards at just the right time.

Fluent in 3 Months (book)

A quick read to get you started on your language learning journey and face any doubts you may have.

Tandem

A language exchange app to meet people who know the language you're seeking and vice versa.

IE Languages

A resource that shows similar languages side by side. For example: English, Spanish, Portuguese, French and Italian vocabulary are in a single table with toggles for each language.

Portuguese - Carioca Connection

Short Portuguese conversations between a Brazilian and American on a wide range of topics.

Spanish - Duolingo Spanish Podcasts

Short stories told with alternating English and Spanish.

Esperanto - lernu!

A free multilingual website dedicated to teaching Esperanto.
