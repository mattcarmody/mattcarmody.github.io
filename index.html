Matt Carmody

Hi, I'm Matt. I'm an engineer, traveler, climber, reader, tinkerer and apsiring polyglot. I value curiousity, resilience and flexibility. While I know that specialization is the path to financial success, I can't help studying a wide range of topics. It's too much fun!

I'm a Midwestern American, born and raised in the suburbs of Chicago. I graduated with a BS in Engineering Mechanics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and worked as an ME co-op and product engineer at Panduit Corp. for two to three years. I then spent the better part of two years backpacking around the world, at times with friends though mostly solo. I trekked to Everest Base Camp in Nepal, sailed on the Puget Sound in Olympia and danced samba in Rio de Janeiro, among so much more! After returning home I learned to program using Python and basic web development w/ HTML, CSS & Javascript. I'm a native English speaker, developed conversational Portuguese in Brazil and accumulated survival Spanish in Spain, Nicaragua & Argentina. I now live in Denver.
