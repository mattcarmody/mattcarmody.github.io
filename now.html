I moved to Denver! Now I'm closer to beautiful landscape and I'm building a new life in a new place. I've moved out of my car and into a house in Washington Park where a friend lives and a bedroom opened up suddenly.

I've been practicing at Anytime Fitness and now in my enormous bedroom where I have plenty of space to exercise, though not quite enough height for handstand training. I expect to soon join Movement Climbing & Fitness, as well as Awaken Gym.

I'm still filling out 'professional' job applications, but not many. I'm happy at the hostel and don't want to leave it for a full time engineering job at the moment. Hopefully I'll be able to build up my skillset to the point where I can take on some part time or freelance programming work and start building up experience.
